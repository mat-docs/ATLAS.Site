## We're Moving Our Support Portal!

We're excited to announce that we're migrating from Zendesk to our new **Motion Applied Support Portal**. This change will provide us with a more integrated and streamlined support experience for all our users.

<!-- more -->

### What's Changing?

Effective immediately, all support requests should be submitted through our new portal:

**New Support Portal URL:** [https://portal.motionapplied.com/Tickets/New](https://portal.motionapplied.com/Tickets/New)

### Why the Change?

- **Better Integration:** The new portal is tightly integrated with Motion Applied systems
- **Improved Experience:** Better ticket tracking and collaboration with Oncar teams.
- **Streamlined Workflows:** Improved organization and categorization of support requests

### What You Need to Do

1. Update your bookmarks to the new portal URL
2. When reporting issues or requesting assistance, use the new [Motion Applied Support Portal](https://portal.motionapplied.com/Tickets/New)
3. Your support tickets and history remain accessible during the transition

### Continued Support

Our team is still here to help! All existing support requests will be transitioned to the new system, and you can reach us through:

- **New Support Portal:** [https://portal.motionapplied.com/Tickets/New](https://portal.motionapplied.com/Tickets/New)
- **Documentation:** Check our comprehensive [documentation](../../) for self-service resources

### Questions?

If you have any questions about this migration or need assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out through our [Support Portal](https://portal.motionapplied.com/Tickets/New).

**Thank you for your continued partnership with ATLAS and Motion Applied!**
