Who we are

ATLAS is the data platform built for you. If you operate at the limit - in motorsport, testing, and advanced engineering - we're built for you.

What we do

We turn complex, high-volume, real-time telemetry into clear, actionable insight - live or post-session.

Why it matters

In high-pressure environments, delayed, misunderstood, or missing data is a form of risk. ATLAS removes that risk.

ATLAS Families

A modular telemetry data platform - not just a viewer. Five capability families cover the full data journey from capture to insight, so you can adopt what you need and scale over time.

📊

Analyse

Visualise, compare, and interrogate high-frequency telemetry - live or post-run. ATLAS Viewer delivers advanced displays, alarms, functions, and virtual parameters so you can make rapid, time-critical decisions.

Best for: analysts, performance engineers, decision speed
🗄️

Store

Keep telemetry organised, durable, and accessible at scale. Session management, bulk import, and database synchronisation via SQL Race Management Studio let distributed teams share consistent datasets.

Best for: data management across users, events, or locations
📡

Stream

Move live telemetry efficiently across people, systems, and sites. Low-latency, multi-client distribution built on modern streaming and microservice architecture for collaboration and remote operations.

Best for: large or distributed teams relying on live data feeds
🔗

Integrate

Connect ATLAS into broader data ecosystems. APIs, Open Data Package, and RTA let you integrate with third-party systems and automate workflows - all without forcing migration.

Best for: software teams, partners, and complex pipelines
🧪

Enrich

Add intelligence on top of raw telemetry. Generate derived parameters, run model-based simulation via vTAG Server, and blend real and virtual data to test scenarios without risk.

Best for: advanced engineering, simulation, and what-if analysis
